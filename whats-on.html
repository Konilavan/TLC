Our ongoing projects aim to preserve Tamil heritage and promote human rights. Key initiatives include:

  • Tamils of Lanka Exhibition: A growing collection of exhibits showcasing Tamil history.
  • Community Education Program: Free workshops and resources for Tamil youth.
